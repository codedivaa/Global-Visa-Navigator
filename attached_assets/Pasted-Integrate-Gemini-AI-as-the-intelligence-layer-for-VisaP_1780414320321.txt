==================================================
GOAL
====

Transform VisaPath from a static assessment into an intelligent immigration advisor.

* Assessment
* Recommendations
* Roadmaps
* Pricing estimates
* AI Advisor

==================================================
ASSESSMENT INTELLIGENCE
=======================

When a user selects:

* Nationality
* Current Country
* Target Country
* Immigration Goal

Use Gemini to generate follow-up questions dynamically.

Example:

Target Country = Japan
Goal = Study

Ask:

* University acceptance status
* Japanese proficiency
* Financial proof
* Intended program

Target Country = Germany
Goal = Work

Ask:

* German proficiency
* Degree recognition
* Occupation
* Years of experience

Target Country = Canada
Goal = PR

Ask:

* IELTS score
* French proficiency
* CRS-related factors

Questions should adapt based on country and goal.
